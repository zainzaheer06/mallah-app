# Mallah

**Compare food prices across delivery apps. Order from the cheapest.**

Mallah helps you find the best price for the meal you actually want — instead of opening HungerStation, Jahez, ToYou, and Keeta one by one to compare.

---

## What it does

- 🔍 **Search** for any restaurant or dish
- 💰 **Compare prices** side-by-side across delivery apps
- 🛒 **Build your cart** and see the total cost (including delivery fees) on each platform
- ⭐ **Save favourites** and get alerts when prices drop
- 📍 **Order from the cheapest** with one tap — Mallah sends you straight to checkout

---

## Why use it

A burger meal can cost SAR 45 on one app and SAR 62 on another for the exact same restaurant. Delivery fees, service charges, and platform-specific promotions vary constantly. Mallah does the comparison for you in seconds.

---

## How it works

1. **Open the app** and tell us your delivery address
2. **Search** for a restaurant or dish, or browse nearby options
3. **Compare** real-time prices across every delivery app available in your area
4. **Tap to order** — we hand you off to the cheapest app, cart pre-filled
